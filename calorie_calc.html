Calorie calculator

How it is calculated?

It's calculated based on Harris-Benedict pattern.

  • BMR for men: 66.5 + (13.75 * weight in kg) + (5.003 * height in cm) - (6.75 * age)
  • BMR for women: 655.1 + (9.563 * weight in kg) + (1.85 * height in cm) - (4.676 * age)

And after that we need to determine the Harris-Benedict equation. To achieve that all you need to do is multiply your BMR by the appropriate activity factor, which is included in higher form and it's called PAL (physical activity level). From first option that said "No physical activity" and that is factor equal 1.2 and reach up to 2.0 in last activity (next activity factor is 0.2 higher than previous one). After that you have in result your TDEE (total daily energy expenditure).

If you want to lose weight from TDEE substract some calories (recommended is reasonable number of 500 kcal/day) will let you lose weight gradually and steadily.
