Calorie Calculator

Calorie calculator is application, that allows you to calculate your daily energy demands, which is mandatory to keep optimal body state and good mood. Knowledge of your energy demands is incredibly precious and important expertise, which will be handy to determine future food plan. Information that you receive after this will be a hint not only for people who wants to lose weight but also for people who wants to get bigger or keep his actual weight.

Calculating energy demands is not as simple as you may think. After that stay complex formula and many variables, which you need to include. In this case is recommended to use calorie calculator. Free application do all the work for you based on few data, which you need to insert in prepared form. Is good to remember that if you give more accurate information you will have better results. Also remember that when you take any diet is highly preferred to consult it with dietician or trainer.

Energy demands

Energy demands is calorie quantity, which are needed for body in daytime to cover energy expenses while maintaining his optimal condition. Quantity of delivered calorie will be different for each person and is dependent of few important conditions. First of all you take a basal metabolic rate(BMR) and overall total daily energy expenditure(TDEE).

Daily calorie body expenses are different and will be higher in situation when physical activity is increased or in convalescence. So when you calculating your (BMR-basal metabolic rate) you need to include:

  • height;
  • weight;
  • age;
  • sex;
  • your metabolism;
  • activity level;
  • current health state
